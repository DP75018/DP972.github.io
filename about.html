About Davon

Paris-born graphic designer crafting cinematic, culture-driven visuals.

Background & Practice

Davon is a Paris-born graphic designer whose creative language draws from cinema, culture, and the power of bold visual storytelling. Now based in Toronto, he moves fluidly between art direction, motion graphics, and illustration, shaping imagery that feels both meticulously crafted and effortlessly expressive.

Visual Language

His aesthetic is anchored in the timeless geometry of Art Deco and the vibrant pulse of the jazzy 1920s–40s. Layered onto this foundation are the electric tones of the 80s, the raw experimental energy of the 90s, and the sleek pop-culture sensibilities of the early 2000s.

This blend of eras forms a signature style that is nostalgic yet unmistakably contemporary.

Approach to Projects

Across commercial projects, brand visuals, and motion-driven concepts, Davon’s work reveals a consistent intention: to create visuals that resonate, shift mood, and tell stories with clarity and emotion.

This portfolio is a curated glimpse into his world — where culture, craft, and narrative come together in striking, cinematic harmony.
